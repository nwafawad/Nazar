About Shilla

Your Trusted Construction Partner

Shilla Construction was established by a group of highly qualified and efficient staff professionals with diversified engineering, industrial, and business backgrounds. Our commitment to excellence drives us to deliver projects that stand the test of time.

Our technical staff is highly experienced in supervising construction works, survey services, and quantities estimation for both small and large-scale projects. We maintain the highest quality standards while coordinating all engineering disciplines professionally.

Based on our advanced project management system, we review and adopt project payments while performing rigorous site tests that ensure quality and accuracy compared to the highest specifications, delivered in record time periods.

Our Specialized Services Include:

  • Construction Supervision - Comprehensive oversight for all sectors we serve
  • Waterproofing Solutions - Optimal protection using the latest waterproofing chemicals
  • Exclusive Partnership - Official agent for DAM Construction Chemicals Co. Ltd products in Port Sudan
