Our Services

Comprehensive construction and chemical solutions for your projects

Construction Supervision

Our highly experienced technical staff provides comprehensive construction supervision services for all sectors we serve. We ensure every project meets the highest standards through:

  • On-site supervision by qualified engineers
  • Regular quality control inspections
  • Progress monitoring and reporting
  • Safety compliance oversight
  • Coordination of all construction activities

Professional Survey Services

Accurate surveying is the foundation of every successful project. Our survey services include:

  • Land and topographic surveys
  • Construction layout and staking
  • As-built surveys
  • Volume calculations
  • GPS and total station surveys

Quantities Estimation

Precise quantity estimation is crucial for project budgeting and planning. We provide detailed estimates for both small and large-scale projects:

  • Material quantities and specifications
  • Labor requirements assessment
  • Cost estimation and budgeting
  • Bill of quantities (BOQ) preparation
  • Value engineering recommendations

Advanced Waterproofing Solutions

We provide optimal protection for buildings from water leakage using the latest waterproofing chemicals and technologies:

  • Basement and foundation waterproofing
  • Roof and terrace waterproofing systems
  • Bathroom and wet area treatments
  • Water tank waterproofing
  • Expansion joint sealing
  • Crystalline waterproofing applications
